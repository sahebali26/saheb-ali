About Me

Rooted in Culture,
Built for the Future

I am Saheb Ali, an entrepreneur from Abhayapuri, Assam, passionate about preserving and celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Assam through authentic traditional jewellery.

I founded Saheb Trend House with a vision to make genuine Assamese jewellery — including iconic pieces for Bihu, weddings, and cultural occasions — accessible to everyone across Assam and all of India.

From local delivery in Abhayapuri, Bongaigaon, Sidli, Manikpur & Boitamari to nationwide shipping via Meesho, we bring tradition to every doorstep.
