About the Brand

Rooted in Assam, Loved Across India

Saheb Trend House was founded by Saheb Ali with a mission to preserve and promote the exquisite craftsmanship of Assamese traditional jewellery. Every piece tells a story of our rich culture.

We specialize in authentic designs for Bihu, weddings, and cultural ceremonies. From classic Golpata bangles to elegant Junbiri sets, our collection is curated for those who appreciate tradition.
