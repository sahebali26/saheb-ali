Who I am

I'm Saheb Ali, a passionate Economics (Honours) student from Assam, deeply engaged in digital entrepreneurship and design. I hold an ADCA (Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications) & IT/ITes-CRM Voice certification, which sparked my interest in tech-driven solutions.

My journey started with Saheb Trend House — an e‑commerce venture on Meesho delivering fashion and lifestyle products. I later expanded into MS Design services specialising in customised almirah printing and interior art. As founder of EDU SMARTSPARK, I share educational resources and guide learners.
